What is photou ?

PhotoU is a Photo sharing website allows users to create their own album upload their photos and allows them to share among group of your friends or among certain groups of people.

Your Account:

How to create an account?
If you are not a member of PhotoU, click on the register link in home page. Fill in the form with your personal details, email id, and password.

How to login?
Enter your registered username and password and click on Sign in to enter to your account.

How to change password?
Go to your profile account settings, click on change password. Enter the current password enter and re- enter the new password.
